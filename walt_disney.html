Walt Disney


The Man who made our childhood awesome!









Here's a timeline of Walt's Life


  • 1901 : Born in Chicago's Hermosa neighbourhood.

  • 1906 : Developed Interest in drawing and was paid to draw the horse of a retired neighbour doctor.

  • 1911 : Moved to Kansas City where he attended Benton Grammar School.

  • 1919 : Worked as an apprentice artist at a commercial art studio.

  • 1920 : Became interested in Animation and started a new company with a co-worker.

  • 1923 : Moved to Hollywood and started Disney Brothers studio with his brother Roy.

  • 1928 : Developed Mickey Mouse, inspired by a pet mouse walt adopted while working.

  • 1937 : Won the Academy award for Animated Short Film.

  • 1948 : Initiated Live action films and won Academy awards in Best Short Subject.

  • 1955 : Opened the Theme park Disneyland and received 20,000 visitors a day.

  • 1965 : Announced plans to develop another theme park to be called DisneyWorld.

  • 1966 : Died at the age of 65.

"All our dreams can come true, if we have the courage to pursue them."
